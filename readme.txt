A central hub for collecting and managing WordPress notifications with multi-channel delivery (Telegram, Email, Slack) and integrations for WooCommerce & Contact Form 7.

== Description ==
Notification Hub aggregates notifications from WordPress core, WooCommerce, and Contact Form 7, and delivers them via multiple channels (Telegram, Email, Slack).  
Ideal for admins who want all alerts in one place with flexible delivery options.

== Installation ==
1. Upload to `/wp-content/plugins/`.
2. Activate the plugin.
3. Go to the **Notifications** menu in your dashboard.
4. Configure settings for Telegram, Email, and Slack under **Notifications > Settings**.
